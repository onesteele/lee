Breach of Contract & Fraud

The Problem: Aggressive Consumer Abuse

A retail client enrolled a customer in their $75,000 premium program. The consumer signed comprehensive contracts with clear terms of service, payment obligations, and arbitration clauses.

Instead of honoring their agreement, the consumer:

  • Falsely claimed the contract was fraudulent
  • Initiated friendly fraud chargebacks
  • Refused all payment obligations
  • Threatened to file false complaints with regulators
Litigation Strategy

Our Response: Total Legal Annihilation

We didn't just defend—we launched a comprehensive counter-attack across multiple legal fronts.

File Immediate Lawsuit

Breach of contract, unjust enrichment, fraud, and tortious interference claims filed in federal court.

Evidence Collection

Discovered signed contracts, recording consent, payment history, and communications proving intentional fraud.

Asset Discovery

Comprehensive investigation revealed hidden assets: primary residence, luxury vehicles, investment accounts.

Pre-Judgment Relief

Secured liens on real property, frozen bank accounts, and seized vehicles pending final judgment.

Case Closed

Complete Victory: Assets Seized, Judgment Enforced
